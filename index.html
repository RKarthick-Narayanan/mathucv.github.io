Professional Summary

Postdoctoral researcher in linguistics with expertise in morphosyntax, ellipsis, and heritage language studies (Dravidian). Bridges formal syntactic theory and empirical fieldwork. Current SNSF-funded project at the University of Lausanne investigates morphosyntactic variation in Swiss Tamil using fieldwork‑based data, sociolinguistics, and typology.

Presented at international venues, contributed to computational linguistics collaborations, and taught core courses (syntax, phonology). Long-term goal: comparative syntax of Dravidian, with focus on ellipsis and null object constructions in typological and heritage contexts.

Keywords: Ellipsis • Null objects • Information structure • Heritage Tamil • Dravidian
